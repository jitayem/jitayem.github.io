Services

These are the variety of services we offer

Women's Hair Cut - $60

Whether you are looking for a bob, layers, or a simple trim, We can do it all!

Color Retouch - $70

Grey coming through at your roots? We will create a custom formula for your hair color making it specifically for you. This will leave you with a seamless blend and acts as if you never had grey hair at all!

Full Balayage - $150

Receive a lived in, dimensional look with this service. This style of handpainting is a low maintance technique meaning you won't need a touch up for a while.

Is your hair lacking tone or feeling brassy? Add this on to your balayage service for your desired tone, or book as a stand-alone service to maintain your color!

Brazilian Blowout $350

Struggling to combat friz? Our Brazilian Blowout treatment helps reduce friz by infusing a keratin complex in your hair leaving it to shine and seal the cuticle for healthier and sleeker hair.

Blowout and Style- $50

Need a style refresh? Book this service as a stand-alone or to the end of your color service to add some movement and bounce to your hair!
